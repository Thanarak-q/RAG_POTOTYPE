# Data Processing Addendum Draft

This draft is for lawyer review.

The shop is the controller. The platform is the processor. The platform processes personal data only to provide chatbot, product-answering, lead, support, and administration features.

The platform will:

- process data under the shop's documented instructions;
- protect data with access controls, encryption, audit logging, and retention controls;
- assist with deletion requests;
- disclose subprocessors;
- notify affected shops within 72 hours of a confirmed personal-data breach where legally required.
